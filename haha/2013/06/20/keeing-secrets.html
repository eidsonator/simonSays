Simon Says

Keeping secrets...

Jun 20, 2013

me: Do you know who I love? Simon: Who? me: I can’t tell you, it’s a secret. Simon: You can tell me, I’m a good secret-keeper. me: Prove it, tell me one secret that you were supposed to keep that you’ve never told anyone. Simon: This one time grandma gave me a cookie, and told me not to tell you. me: Oh, really? Simon: Yeah, really, see, I’m a good secret-keeper!
